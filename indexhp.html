Who is RAJA SOLIMAN

Raja Soliman, also known as Raja Sulayman, was a 16th-century Muslim ruler of the Kingdom of Maynila, located in present-day Manila, Philippines. He is renowned for his strong leadership and resistance against Spanish colonization, particularly during the Battle of Bangkusay in 1571, where he fought to defend his domain from foreign conquest. A symbol of bravery and patriotism, Raja Soliman is remembered in Philippine history as a leader who stood firm in protecting his people and culture against colonial forces. His legacy as a symbol of patriotism and leadership lives on in institutions like Raja Soliman Science and Technology High School in Binondo, Manila.

What We Offer

Raja Soliman Science and Technology High School offers a curriculum focused on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM), providing students with a strong foundation in these critical fields. The school aims to nurture innovation, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills, preparing students for future careers in science and technology. The school is committed to excellence, encouraging students to become responsible, skilled, and globally competitive individuals.
